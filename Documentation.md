## Book Choice & Justification
The book I chose to create my webiste around is Pride & Prejudice (1813) by Jane Austen.
Pride & Prejudice is a classic romantic novel set in the 19th Century rural England, revolving around the Mr and Mrs Bennet and their five daughters. The eldest, Jane is optimistic, kind, and patient and known for her beauty. Elizabeth the second eldest, is independent, playful, and intelligent, she is the main character in Pride and Prejudice. Catherine, Lydia and Mary are the last three sisters. Mrs Bennet is irrational, foolish and determined to find all of her daughters marriages. Mr Bennet on the other hand is intelligent, scarcastic but a weak and indolent father. Mr Bingeley is a wealthy, cheerful, polite and well-mannered gentlemen who falls in love with Jane immediately on sight as he moved to Netherfield Park from London. Mr Darcy is Mr Bingeley's wealthy, arrogant, intelligent, haughty but loyal friend.
