## Book Choice & Justification
The book I chose to create my webiste around is Pride & Prejudice (1813) by Jane Austen.
Pride & Prejudice is a classic romantic novel set in the 19th Century rural England, revolving around the Mr and Mrs Bennet and their five daughters. The eldest, Jane is optimistic, kind, and patient and known for her beauty. Elizabeth the second eldest, is independent, beautiful, playful, and intelligent, she is the main character in Pride and Prejudice. Catherine, Lydia and Mary are the last three sisters. Mrs Bennet is irrational, foolish and nosy. Mr Bennet on the other hand is intelligent, scarcastic but a weak and indolent father. Mr Bingeley is a wealthy, cheerful, polite and well-mannered gentlemen. Mr Darcy is Mr Bingeley's wealthy, arrogant, intelligent, haughty but loyal friend. Pride and Prejudce is centered around the Bennet sisters need to get married for financial security. Upon moving to Netherfield Park from London Mr Bingely immediately falls in love with Jane at a ball, who returns the sentiment. Mr Darcy refuses to dance with Elizabteth on anyone outside his social circle. However, in later occaasions Mr Darcy finds himself attracted to Elizabeth. Throughout this time Elizabeth rejects Mr Collin's hand and becomes charmed by Mr Wickman.
