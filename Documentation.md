Mrs Bennet is irrational, foolish and nosy. Mr Bennet on the other hand is intelligent, scarcastic but a weak and indolent father. Mr Bingeley is a wealthy, cheerful, polite and well-mannered gentlemen. Mr Darcy is Mr Bingeley's wealthy, arrogant, intelligent, haughty but loyal friend. Pride and Prejudce is centered around the Bennet sisters need to get married for financial security. Mr Darcy refuses to dance with Elizabteth on anyone outside his social circle. However, in later occaasions Mr Darcy finds himself attracted to Elizabeth.
